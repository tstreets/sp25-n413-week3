== Description ==

The Educator Academy WordPress Theme is a comprehensive and dynamic solution designed to create modern and professional education websites, tailored for online education, distance learning, and virtual classrooms. Perfect for K-12 education, higher education, online academies, language schools, corporate training programs, and educational institutions, this theme transforms any website into a powerful digital learning hub. It simplifies the creation and management of a Learning Management System (LMS) for schools, universities, online academies, tutoring centers, and education consultants, making it easier to deliver online courses, webinars, workshops, and educational content. With integrated eCommerce tools and payment gateways, you can easily monetize your educational services, such as online courses, membership subscriptions, and digital resources. The theme also features advanced scheduling tools for managing virtual classrooms, live lessons, group study sessions, and one-on-one tutoring appointments, enhancing the student experience with an intuitive education management system. The modern, clean design of the Educator Academy WordPress Theme emphasizes educational content, with customizable layouts and pre-designed templates for course landing pages, academic websites, education blogs, student portals, and faculty profiles, offering flexibility to create a unique online presence. Fully mobile-responsive, the theme ensures your educational resources are accessible across devices, fostering better engagement in online programs and courses. Additionally, it supports popular educational plugins like BuddyPress, LearnDash, and WPForms, enabling community features, quizzes, assessments, and interactive forms. Whether you run an online academy, a fitness school, or a certification program, this theme provides everything you need to manage and grow your educational website. With built-in SEO optimization, the theme helps you rank higher on search engines, broadening your audience reach and improving engagement with students.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
